# ⚡ My Command Center
### Your Personal Daily Command Centre — Built for Busy Lives

---

## What Is This App?

My Command Center is a personal productivity and wellness app built for people who manage a lot — work, family, health, and personal goals — all at once. It brings your entire day into one place: your schedule, your family's appointments, your health habits, your prayers, and your personal vision. No switching between apps. No missed appointments. No forgotten habits.

It runs in your browser and can be installed on your phone like a real app — no App Store required.

---

## Who Is It For?

This app was designed for busy professionals, parents, and caregivers who want to:

- See today, tomorrow, and the week ahead at a glance
- Track family appointments colour-coded by person
- Stay on top of daily health and wellness habits
- Never miss a prayer time
- Start every morning with an AI-generated briefing of the day ahead

---

## Core Features

### 📅 Schedule Views
- **Today** — your full day in 30-minute blocks with colour-coded family events and live prayer time markers. A live dot shows your current time block.
- **Tomorrow** — a clean card view of everything scheduled for the next day, including prayer times.
- **Week** — a full 7-day overview with all family events visible at once.

### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Tracking
Each family member gets their own colour and emoji. Their events appear colour-coded throughout the app so you always know at a glance whose appointment is whose. Works with one Google Calendar account — just include the person's name in the event title and the app assigns it automatically.

### ☀️ AI Morning Briefing
Every morning, tap Generate and the app reads your schedule, prayer times, and daily intentions to produce a warm personalised summary of your day — what's coming, what matters, and one motivating sentence to start strong.

### ⚡ Quick Add
Add events instantly by typing or speaking. The AI reads your natural language — *"Khursheed has a cardiology appointment Thursday at 2pm at Rockyview"* — and extracts all the details automatically. One tap to confirm and it appears on your schedule.

### 🌿 Wellness Tracker
A full wellness dashboard with eight dedicated areas:

| Tab | What It Tracks |
|---|---|
| 🌿 Habits | 30-min break, walk, eat on time — tap to check off |
| 🫧 Fluids | Daily fluid intake toward your 2L goal |
| 🏋️ Exercise | Weekly exercise routines with day tracking and progress bars |
| 💊 Supplements | Morning supplements (Omega-3, Vitamin D, Calcium, Berberine, Insulinol) and night supplement (Magnesium) |
| 🌙 Sleep | Bedtime and wake time targets with Fajr adjustment note |
| 🕌 Prayers | Live Hanafi prayer times for your location with countdown to next prayer |
| 🌆 Wind-Down | Evening checklist to prepare for a restful night |
| 🍽️ Meals | Meal timing log with post-meal walk reminders |

### 📊 Daily Score
A live wellness score (0–100) calculated from your habits, fluids, exercise, supplements, prayers, and wind-down routine. Updates in real time as you check things off throughout the day.

### 🎯 Vision Board
A personal space for your goals, affirmations, and vision images. Your goals are included in your AI morning briefing so you see them every single day before your schedule loads.

### 🗓️ Calendar Sync
Connect your Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook to pull real appointments into the app automatically. Supports multiple accounts — personal, work, and family. Smart name detection means one Google account can track the whole family.

## How To Use It Daily

**Morning (5 minutes)**
1. Open the app → tap **✨ Generate** for your morning briefing
2. Review **📅 Today** to see your full schedule
3. Check off any prayers already completed
4. Log your first fluid of the day

**Throughout the day**
- Tap habits off as you complete them in the sidebar
- Log fluids with one tap in Wellness → Fluids
- Use **+ Add** to quickly add new events by voice or text
- Check your **📊 Score** to see how the day is building

**Evening (3 minutes)**
- Work through **🌆 Wind-Down** checklist
- Log your night supplement
- Review **🌅 Tomorrow** so nothing surprises you

## Installing on Your Phone

This app works as a Progressive Web App — it installs directly from your browser with no App Store needed.

**iPhone:** Open the app URL in Safari → tap the Share button → tap **Add to Home Screen** → tap Add

**Android:** Open the app URL in Chrome → tap the three-dot menu → tap **Install App**

The app will appear on your home screen with its own icon, open full screen, and behave exactly like a native app.

---

## Privacy & Data

- All your personal data (events, habits, family members, goals) is stored **only on your device** using browser storage
- Nothing is sent to any server except when you use AI features (morning briefing, quick add parsing)
- AI features use Anthropic's Claude API — only the event list and your prompt are sent, never personal health data
- Your API key is stored locally on your device only
